WHO ARE WE?

We are a healthy meal prep service that focuses on serving individuals with cognitive and developmental disabilities in the Phoenix area. We deliver to the day programs (DTA, DTT, DTS), and employment centers (CBE).

WHY DO WE DO THIS?

After years of experience working in this field, we have identified a nutritional need which led us to develop a service that provides healthy and tasty meals.

Our goal is to contribute to the health and well being of this community.
